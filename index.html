pcb-motor

Design a motor that IS a circuit board.

A coreless axial-flux motor where the stator is just… a PCB. This repo takes you from “roughly this much torque in roughly this much space” to Gerber-ready KiCad files — with a real Biot–Savart engine underneath and a feasibility gate that would rather shout now than after you've ordered boards. These pages are what pcb-motor showcase generates: fully self-contained, every polygon and field sample computed from the design.
